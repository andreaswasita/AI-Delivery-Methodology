üìö 3-Tier Learning Model

Tier 1: AI Literacy

8 hours All Employees

Goal: Universal AI awareness and responsible use

  • What is AI? (Gen AI, ML, Deep Learning)
  • AI in Our Organization
  • AI Ethics & Responsible Use
  • Prompt Engineering Basics
  • AI Tools Available to You

Certification: "AI Literacy Certified" digital badge

Tier 2: AI Practitioner

40-60 hours 30-40% of org

Goal: Job-specific AI skills for daily productivity

8 Specialized Tracks:

  • Business Leadership (40 hrs)
  • Product & Design (50 hrs)
  • Engineering (60 hrs)
  • Data Science (60 hrs)
  • Marketing & Sales (45 hrs)
  • Operations & Finance (40 hrs)
  • Customer Support (50 hrs)
  • IT & Admin (55 hrs)

Certification: "[Track Name] AI Practitioner Certified"

Tier 3: AI Specialist

120-200 hours 5-10% of org

Goal: Deep expertise in AI development and architecture

5 Specializations:

  • AI Solution Architect (150 hrs)
  • AI Engineer / Developer (140 hrs)
  • MLOps Engineer (160 hrs)
  • Data Engineer for AI (150 hrs)
  • AI Governance & Ethics (120 hrs)

Certification: "[Specialization] AI Specialist Certified"

Aligns with Microsoft certifications (AZ-305, AI-102, DP-100)

üèÜ Certification Levels

Level 1: AI Literacy Certified

Requirements:

  • Complete AI-101 course
  • Pass 20-question assessment (80%)

Validity: 1 year

Renewal: 2-hour refresher + assessment

Recognition: Digital badge for email/LinkedIn

Level 2: AI Practitioner Certified

Requirements:

  • Complete all track courses (40-60 hours)
  • Pass comprehensive exam (85%)
  • Complete capstone project (80%+)

Validity: 2 years

Renewal: 20 hours continuing education

Recognition: Digital badge + printed certificate

Level 3: AI Specialist Certified

Requirements:

  • Complete specialization (120-200 hours)
  • Pass comprehensive exam (90%)
  • Complete major capstone (85%+)
  • Peer review & presentation

Validity: 1 year

Renewal: 40 hours + re-certification

Recognition: Premium badge + framed certificate

üåü Master AI Architect

Requirements:

  • Hold 2+ AI Specialist certifications
  • Lead 3+ successful AI projects
  • Contribute to academy (teach/create)
  • Published thought leadership
  • Peer nominated & director approved

Validity: Lifetime (with annual contribution)

Recognition:

  • Master certification ceremony
  • Listed on Master Faculty roster
  • $5K annual conference budget
  • Eligible to review capstone projects
