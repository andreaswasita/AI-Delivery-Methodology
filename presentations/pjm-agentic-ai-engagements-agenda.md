### **Module 3: Pre-Sales - Vision to Business Case** *(20 minutes)*

**Time:** 0:25 - 0:45

**Objectives:**
- Structure AI discovery conversations
- Quantify value and build business cases
- Identify red flags early

**Content:**

**3.1 Discovery Framework** (7 min)
**The 5 Discovery Questions Every PM Must Ask:**

1. **Vision:** "What business problem are we solving?"
   - Not "We want AI" but "We want to reduce churn by 25%"
   - Technique: Five Whys Analysis

2. **Success Metrics:** "How do we measure success?"
   - Must be quantifiable: revenue, cost, time, quality
   - Example: "$2M annual savings" not "better customer service"

3. **Data Reality:** "What data do we have, and what shape is it in?"
   - 80% of AI project time is data prep
   - Red flag: "We have lots of data" but can't describe it
   - Green flag: "Here's our data dictionary and quality report"

4. **Change Readiness:** "Who needs to change how they work?"
   - AI success = Tech adoption + Process change + People buy-in
   - Map stakeholders: Champions, Resisters, Neutrals

5. **Constraints:** "What are our non-negotiables?"
   - Compliance requirements (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)
   - Budget and timeline realities
   - Technical debt and integrations

**Content:**

**4.1 The 8-Phase AI Delivery Methodology** (10 min)

**Phase 0: Pre-Sales & Discovery** (2-4 weeks)
- Discovery workshops
- Data assessment
- Business case development
- **PM Deliverables:** SOW, Project Charter, Initial Backlog

**Phase 1: Mobilization** (1-2 weeks)
- Team formation
- Environment setup
- Stakeholder kickoff
- **PM Deliverables:** RACI Matrix, Communication Plan, Risk Register

**Phase 2: Hackathons & Proof of Value** (2-3 weeks)
- Rapid prototyping (1-2 week sprints)
- Validate technical feasibility
- Test with real data
- **PM Deliverables:** POV Report, Go/No-Go Decision

**Phase 3: Platform Setup** (2-4 weeks)
- Azure AI infrastructure
- Security & compliance
- CI/CD pipelines
- **PM Deliverables:** Technical Design Doc, Security Sign-off

**Phase 4: Build** (4-8 weeks)
- Agent development (2-week sprints)
- Integration with existing systems
- **PM Deliverables:** Sprint Reports, Burndown Charts, Velocity Tracking

**Phase 5: Integration** (2-3 weeks)
- System integration testing
- User acceptance testing
- **PM Deliverables:** Test Reports, UAT Sign-off

**Phase 6: Test & Evaluate** (2-3 weeks)
- Performance testing
- Security testing
- Business metric validation
- **PM Deliverables:** Test Summary, Defect Log

**Phase 7: Prepare & Deploy** (1-2 weeks)
- Production deployment
- User training
- Runbook creation
- **PM Deliverables:** Deployment Plan, Training Materials, Runbook

**Phase 8: Operate & Care** (Ongoing)

- User feedback loops
- **PM Deliverables:** Operations Dashboard, Monthly KPI Reports

### **Module 6: Real-World Case Studies** *(8 minutes)*

**Time:** 1:20 - 1:28

**Objectives:**
- Learn from successful AI engagements
- Understand what "good" looks like
- Apply lessons learned

**Content:**

**Case Study 1: Healthcare AI - Diagnostic Agent** (4 min)

**Client:** 8-hospital health system  
**Problem:** Diagnostic turnaround time 48-72 hours  
**Solution:** Medical imaging diagnostics agent  

**PM Success Factors:**
- **Clear Metrics:** Reduce turnaround to <24 hours, maintain 95%+ accuracy
- **Phased Approach:** Pilot in 1 hospital, then scale to 8
- **Change Management:** 40 hours of radiologist training, workflow redesign
- **Risk Management:** Human-in-the-loop for edge cases

**Results:**
- 67% faster diagnostic turnaround (72hr ‚Üí 24hr)
- 95.3% diagnostic accuracy (validated against expert radiologists)
- 281% ROI in Year 1
- $7.88M annual net benefit

**PM Lessons:**
- Started with POV (3 weeks) before full commitment
- Invested 20% of budget in change management (critical!)
- Built trust through transparency (model explainability)
- Celebrated quick wins to build momentum

**Case Study 2: Financial Services - Fraud Detection Agent** (4 min)

**Client:** Digital banking platform  
**Problem:** $4.2M annual fraud losses, 45% false positive rate  
**Solution:** Real-time fraud detection agent with adaptive learning  

**PM Success Factors:**
- **Data Quality:** 6-week data sprint before model development
- **Compliance First:** Legal and compliance review in Phase 0
- **Continuous Testing:** 4-week testing phase (vs typical 2 weeks)
- **Gradual Rollout:** 10% ‚Üí 50% ‚Üí 100% traffic over 6 weeks

**Results:**
- 94% reduction in fraud losses ($4.2M ‚Üí $250K)
- 89% reduction in false positives
- 1,203% ROI in Year 1
- $24.76M annual net benefit

**PM Lessons:**
- Don't skip data quality - it's 80% of success
- Over-invest in testing for high-risk applications
- Gradual rollout reduces production risk
- Monitor closely for first 60 days post-deployment

**Key Takeaways:**
- Both projects followed the 8-phase methodology
- Both invested heavily in change management
- Both used phased/gradual deployment approaches
- Both had clear, measurable success criteria from Day 1
