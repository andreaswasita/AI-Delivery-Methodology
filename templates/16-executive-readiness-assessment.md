## Assessment Purpose

This assessment evaluates executive and organizational readiness for AI transformation across five critical dimensions:

1. **Strategic Clarity** - Clear vision and alignment
2. **Executive Commitment** - Leadership engagement and support
3. **Decision-Making Readiness** - Ability to make AI-specific decisions
4. **Risk Tolerance** - Comfort with AI risks and governance
5. **Change Leadership** - Capability to drive organizational transformation

**Scoring**: Each dimension scored 1-5 (25 points total)
- **20-25 points**: Strong readiness - proceed with confidence
- **15-19 points**: Moderate readiness - address gaps before scaling
- **10-14 points**: Low readiness - significant coaching/preparation needed
- **Below 10**: Not ready - defer until fundamentals addressed

## Assessment Discussion Guide

### How to Present Results to Executive Sponsor

**Frame positively and constructively:**

> "Thank you for the time to assess your readiness for AI initiatives. I've completed a comprehensive evaluation across five dimensions. Here's what I found..."

**Structure the conversation:**

**1. Celebrate Strengths** (5 min)
- Start with highest-scoring dimensions
- Acknowledge what they're doing well
- Build confidence

**2. Identify Opportunities** (10 min)
- Present gaps as opportunities, not failures
- Show how closing gaps increases success probability
- Reference common patterns across organizations

**3. Recommend Path Forward** (10 min)
- Present clear recommendation
- Show how coaching will help
- Outline timeline and commitment needed

**4. Secure Agreement** (5 min)
- Get buy-in on assessment
- Agree on coaching plan
- Schedule follow-up sessions

**Key Messages:**
- "This assessment shows you're [readiness level]—here's what that means..."
- "The gaps I've identified are common and addressable..."
- "With [specific actions], we can move from [current] to [target] readiness..."
- "Organizations that invest in this preparation have much higher success rates..."
- "I'm committed to coaching you through this journey..."
