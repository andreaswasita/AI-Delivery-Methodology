## Part 2: Business Challenges (20 minutes)

### 2.1 Top Pain Points

**Instructions:** Identify 3-5 specific business problems or pain points in your domain. Be as specific as possible with measurable impacts.

**Note:** For each challenge, please complete a "Five Whys" analysis to help us understand the root cause. The Five Whys technique involves asking "why" repeatedly (typically 5 times) to drill down from symptoms to root causes. This will help us design AI solutions that address fundamental issues, not just surface symptoms.

**Five Whys Example:**
- **Problem:** Customer onboarding takes 45 days
- **Why?** → Multiple manual approval steps required
- **Why are manual approvals needed?** → Risk assessment must be done manually
- **Why must it be manual?** → No integrated system to access customer data
- **Why no integrated system?** → Legacy systems don't communicate
- **Why don't they communicate?** → No API integration infrastructure exists
- **Root Cause:** Lack of system integration infrastructure
