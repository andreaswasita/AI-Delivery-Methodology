## 1. Communication Strategy

### 1.1 Communication Objectives
- Keep all stakeholders informed of project progress, risks, and decisions
- Build and maintain stakeholder engagement and support
- Manage expectations and address concerns proactively
- Celebrate successes and build momentum
- Facilitate effective team collaboration
- Enable timely decision-making

### 1.2 Communication Principles
- **Timely**: Information shared when it's needed
- **Relevant**: Tailored to audience needs
- **Accurate**: Factual and verified
- **Clear**: Easy to understand, jargon-free when possible
- **Consistent**: Regular cadence and format
- **Two-way**: Encourage feedback and dialogue
- **Transparent**: Honest about challenges and risks

## 7. Crisis Communication Plan

### 7.1 Crisis Definition
A crisis includes:
- Critical system failure affecting production
- Security breach or data leak
- Major project delay (>1 month)
- Budget overrun (>20%)
- Key resource loss
- Scope cancellation risk

### 7.2 Crisis Communication Protocol

**Step 1: Immediate Response (Within 1 hour)**
- PM assesses situation
- Notify Executive Sponsor
- Convene crisis team

**Step 2: Initial Communication (Within 4 hours)**
- Notify steering committee
- Brief communication to affected stakeholders
- Establish war room/crisis channel

**Step 3: Regular Updates**
- Daily updates until resolved
- Honest and transparent messaging
- Clear action plans

**Step 4: Post-Crisis**
- Root cause analysis
- Lessons learned
- Process improvements

## 11. Communication Checklist

### Before Each Communication
- [ ] Is the message clear and concise?
- [ ] Is it tailored to the audience?
- [ ] Is it timely?
- [ ] Have I checked for accuracy?
- [ ] Is the tone appropriate?
- [ ] Is there a clear call-to-action if needed?
- [ ] Have I considered sensitivities?

### After Each Communication
- [ ] Track who responded/engaged
- [ ] Follow up on action items
- [ ] Gather feedback
- [ ] Update communication log

## 12. Special Situations

### 12.1 Bad News Communication
- Don't delay - share quickly
- Be honest and transparent
- Explain impact
- Present mitigation plan
- Show path forward
- Take ownership

### 12.2 Celebrating Success
- Recognize team contributions
- Share wins broadly
- Connect to business value
- Thank stakeholders
- Build momentum

### 12.3 Managing Rumors
- Address quickly
- Provide facts
- Be transparent about what you can't share
- Establish yourself as reliable source
